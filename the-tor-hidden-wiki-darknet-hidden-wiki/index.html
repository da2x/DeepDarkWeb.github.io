The Tor Hidden Wiki - Darknet Hidden Wiki

The Hidden Wiki is the name of several censorship-resistant "wikis" hosted as Tor hidden services / onion services that serve as a directory of links to other .onion sites.

Anyone can anonymously edit a Hidden Wiki after registering on the site and add their own onion site / hidden service to the directory. Also anyone can easily host a Hidden Wiki themselves on the Tor hidden service network.

The first Hidden Wiki was probably created in 2007/2008, to help users find their way to "important" and "useful" websites on the darknet.

The following link will, with utilizing a Tor2Web proxy, take you to an embedded version of a Tor Hidden Wiki currently hosted as an onion service / hidden service under the onion URL http://zqktlwi4fecvo6ri.onion.

Accessing any of these onion sites will NOT be anonymous, if you are not connected to the Tor network  and access The Hidden Wiki onion URL (http://zqktlwi4fecvo6ri.onion) directly. 

Note: You should also keep in mind that, since anyone one can edit such a "Hidden Wiki", lots of the onion sites / hidden services listed are just scams, frauds, nonsense, phishing attempts (especially when it comes to Tor Marketplace / Darknet Market listings!!) and/or host/offer illegal content/services.

This Hidden Wiki is just for educational purposes embedded here (not hosted).
It is strongly recommended to rather install the Tor Browser first and then visit the onion address http://zqktlwi4fecvo6ri.onion directly.
